Andra Steele's About Me Page

My name is Andra and I'm a 29 year old aspiring developer based in Portland, Oregon.

I went to university in Austin and initially went for piano performance. I ended up graduating with a Sociology degree, every intention to go to law school, and absolutely no desire to follow through.

I began cooking professionally and worked as a chef for the next six years. I was laid off of my restaurant job once COVID took hold of the hospitality industry, and began coding as a means to learn something different.

I currently work as a sales associate for a USDA processing facility. I know, glamorous. I still work with chefs and I still work with food. I get to have conversations with people all day, every day and I don't think that's a bad gig. My favorite things to do are still cook and think about food, but now I get to do it for fun with my partner and pup. Also not a bad gig.
